                Written By
                Adelle Fernandes
            
            MTV Video Music Awards 2022

                        The nominations for the much-awaited MTV Video Music Awards 2022 were announced 
                        on July 26, and fans cheered and rooted for their favourite artists to win. 
                        Artists including Jack Harlow, Kendrick Lamar, Lil Nas X, Doja Cat, Harry Styles 
                        and others dominated the list, as reported by Deadline, putting them in contention 
                        to bag several awards. The MTV Video Music Awards 2022 are scheduled to take place 
                        on August 28, and it would be interesting for viewers to see which artistes bag 
                        the top honours.
